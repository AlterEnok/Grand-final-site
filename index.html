SUPER TROFEO WORLD FINAL

The Lamborghini World Final has concluded each Super Trofeo season since 2013. Each year, drivers from all continental championships travel to a different circuit and compete in two 50-minute sprint races to determine the overall winner as the Lamborghini World Champion. The Lamborghini World Finals is not only the biggest race weekend of the year, but also a true festival of motorsport that attracts fans from all over the world.

An owners parade, previews and paddock entertainment for fans are just some of the activities organized for the event.

SUPER TROPHY TEAMS

All the cars that compete in the Lamborghini Super Trofeo championships are driven by private teams who use their experience to fine-tune them for the drivers. There are driver and team ratings for each Lamborghini Super Trofeo Continental Championship.

The drivers and teams in the Continental Series compete against each other each year in the Lamborghini Grand Final, the event that concludes the Super Trofeo sporting season. Two qualifying sessions and two races determine the series' world champions. Each year the Grand Final is held at a different circuit and in past editions it has already touched the European, Asian and American continents.
